About 关于

I'm a designer working across typography, identity, and the quiet spaces between Eastern and Western visual traditions.

My practice lives at the intersection of 历史 and craft — digitizing handwritten ledgers from 1970s rural China, reconstructing lost letterforms, designing marks that honor what came before.

Currently open to collaborations in book design, brand identity, and archival projects.
